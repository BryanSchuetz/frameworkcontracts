Support to the establishment of the State Aid Control System

The overall objective is to support the establishment of an effective State aid control system in Ukraine as required under the EU-Ukraine Association agreement.

The specific objective of the assignment is to assist the Antimonopoly Committee of Ukraine to establish a State aid control system by 2 August 2017 in accordance with the Law of Ukraine on State aid to undertakings. This assignment will provide “bridging assistance” until a dedicated technical assistance project can take over the work sometime in the third quarter of 2017. The specific issues that require immediate assistance have been identified in section 2.3 below.

Category I Expert on EU State Aid law and Team Leader

Qualifications and skills:

  • At least Master’s Degree in law, economics, business administration or public administration. Alternatively, a minimum of 7 years of relevant experience in the field of competition law, in addition to the minimum number of years of general professional experience required below.

General professional experience:

  • At least 12 years of professional experience in the field or law, economics or public administration.

Specific professional experience:

  • Preferably 5 years (with a minimum of 3 years) within the last 15 years of experience in the field of state aid gained in an EU institution, a public authority in an EU Member State, an EEA country, a country operating a fully functioning State aid regulatory system or in a pre-accession country to the EU or an EU candidate country;
  • Preferably 2 years (with minimum 1 year) of experience in establishing, implementing or operating a State aid control and monitoring system in line with EU rules;
  • Experience in implementing technical assistance projects would be an asset;

Language skills

  • Command of English (at least B2 level);
  • Command of Ukrainian or Russian would be an advantage.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to Aurelie_Durand@dai.com
