Evaluation of a World Bank group managed project entitled ‘Single Donor Trust Fund for the Decentralized Service Delivery Project in Sierra Leone’ (Contract No. FED/2012/284-400)

Global objective

The overall objective of this contract is to provide an ex-post evaluation of the project.

Specific objective(s)

The main objective of the evaluation is to provide the Delegation of European Union with:

  • an overall independent assessment of the implementation of the project (Decentralized Service Delivery Project in Sierra Leone FED/2012/284-400), paying particularly attention to the results of the project against its objectives;
  • key lessons and recommendations in order to improve current and future action in similar areas

Requested services

The mission will include one Senior Expert (Category II) for this assignment. It is to be divided into three distinctive phases i.e. Phase I: Inception Phase, Phase II: Field and Synthesis Phase, Phase III: Submission phase. Deliverables in the form of reports and/or slide presentations will be submitted at the end of the corresponding stages.

Along with the evaluation of the results of the project, the consultant will assess the effectiveness, the sustainability and the impact of the project on its target areas and groups.

In addition, the evaluation will assess the following:

  • the added value of the project, both regarding its design and implementation;
  • the coherence of the project itself, with the EU strategy in Sierra Leone

Activities will include:

  • verifying if the project is felt at district council level;
  • targeted evaluation of sectors Health, Sanitation, Education and Rural Water, Solid Waste Management and Social Assistance Service Sectors, assessing the results against the specific objectives of the project;
  • assessment of the Integrated Project Administration Unit’s (IPAU), institutional and technical capacity including procurement and financial management, monitoring, evaluation and reporting;
  • verification of the results stated in the various reports of the program ensuring they match with the reality on the ground;
  • Assessment of visibility aspects against EU requirements.
