Assessment and Review of the General and Specific conditions of ENPARD SRC for the second and third variable tranches

Global objective

To assess the fulfilment of the general and specific conditions for the second and third tranche of the ENPARD SRC, as stipulated in the Annex 1 of the Financing Agreement ENI/2014/034-128.

Specific objective(s)

  • To assess the compliance with the SRC general condition on Public Policy in the sector, as stipulated in the Annex 1 of the Financing Agreement ENI/2014/034-128, page 10;
  • To assess and value the degree of attainment of the performance indicators of ENPARD SRC specific conditions for the second variable tranche (2015 and 2016 performance indicators, as stipulated in the Annex 1 of the Financing Agreement ENI/2014/034-128, page 11-15) and third variable tranche (2017 performance indicators, as stipulated in the Annex 1 of the Financing Agreement ENI/2014/034-128, page 16-19);
  • To provide additional analytical ENPARD SRC information, examples, including justifications and evidence, and assessment services in order to allow the relevant European Union services to embed the assessment of the ENPARD SRC general and specific conditions in a broader context.

Project duration: 440 days
