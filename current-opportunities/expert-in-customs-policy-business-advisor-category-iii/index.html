Expert in Customs policy/Business Advisor - Category III

  • Project: Technical Assistance to the Customs Administration of the Republic of Serbia for the drafting Terms of Reference for IPA 2013
  • Lot: BENEF 10
  • Assignment Duration: 40 working days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/338468 - Version 2
  • Location: Serbia, Belgrade
  • Estimated Start Date: 4 September 2014
  • Application Deadline: 26 August 2014

Global objective:

The global objective of this assignment is to improve the quality of the services of the SCA and to support modernisation and improvement of the functioning of SCA through strengthening its administrative and operational capacity.

Specific objective:

The specific objective of this assignment is to provide technical assistance to the Department for Contracting and Financing of EU Funded projects (CFCU - Central Finance and Contracting Unit) and the beneficiary (Customs Administration of the Republic of Serbia - SCA) by developing the Terms of Reference for the implementation of service contract „Technical Assistance for Establishing the Institutional Framework for the Implementation of AIS/AES” to be financed under IPA 2013.

Expert in Customs policy/Business advisor - Category III

Qualifications and skills :

  • University degree in a relevant discipline (e.g. Law, Economics, Public administration, Business Administration or other areas relevant to the project).

  • Excellent knowledge of English

  • Computer literacy

General professional experience:

Required: Minimum three (3) years of professional experience in IT systems implementation for a Customs Administration in a EU member State (only counting experience after accession of a country to the EU) or related European Commission Services.

Specific professional experience:

  • Previous experience in at least one project in development of IT systems in the field of the AIS and AES in EU Member States (only counting experience after accession of a country to the EU), European Commission Services or candidate countries(only counting experience after a country received candidate status);
  • Experience in Customs business processes and complex system integration in at least one project of such type;
  • Experience of providing Technical Assistance in IPA countries covered by article 19 of IPA regulation in at least one project.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to Agnese_Ortolani@dai.com
