Expert in Local Development/Local Governance – Category II

  • Project: Evaluation of European Commission's External Aid Programmes funded in the field of Local Development
  • Lot: BENEF 7
  • Assignment Duration: 5 months
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2015/357974 - Version 1
  • Location: Lebanon
  • Estimated Start Date: May 2015
  • Application Deadline: 17th March 2015

With the aim to provide the Lebanese Authorities, beneficiaries of the EU funded programmes with an overall, independent and accountable result-oriented Ex-post Evaluation of :

  • “ Appui au développement local dans le nord du Liban” – ENPI/2008/19-623 – Component Community Development : a selection of 5 projects implemented by the European Social Development Fund (ESFD);
  • “ Support for economic recovery I : Local development, reconstruction, infrastructure rehabilitation” – ENPI/2007/18-882 – Component Local Development Projects : the 12 projects implemented by the Unions of Municipalities (UoM);
  • “ Local Authorities in Development” : a selection of 6 projects.

The objective of this assignment is threefold:

  • To assess the implementation of the interventions, paying a particular attention to the results of the project against its objectives;
  • To assess the impact of the programmes compared to the initial objectives;
  • To propose key lessons and recommendations for the Lebanese Authorities to prepare a strategy and design orientation interms of support to local development and for the future EU interventions in order to improve current and upcoming actions in the field of local development/local governance in Lebanon.

Expert in Local Development/Local Governance – Category II

Qualifications and skills:

At least Masters Degree in economics, social science, or equivalent; in its absence equivalent professional experience of 10 years.

General professional experience:

  • Experience in implementing programmes aiming at improving local governance is compulsory;
  • At least six (6) years of experience in sectors related to lot 7 (i.e. technical description), in addition to the minimum expertise common to all lots, is compulsory.

Specific professional experience:

  • Experience as evaluator in the local development field in at least 1 project/programme;
  • Experience as evaluator in the local development field of an EU funded project/programme is an asset;
  • Experience in Mediterranean countries and knowledge of Middle East political context;
  • Knowledge of EU project cycle management, EU procedures.

Soft skills:

Excellent writing and editing skills.

Language skills:

Excellent command of Arabic and English is compulsory;

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to Adeline_VanHoutte@dai.com
