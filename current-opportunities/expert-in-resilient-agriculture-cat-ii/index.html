Expert in Resilient Agriculture (cat II)

  • Project: Formulation of the Climate Change and Research (REACH) Programme
  • Lot: BENEF 6
  • Assignment Duration: 42 working days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2017/383302 - Version 1
  • Location: Ghana
  • Estimated Start Date: 13th March (30th June 2017)

Formulation of the Climate Change and Research (REACH) Programme

The overall objective of this mission is to support the formulation of the REACH programme, a component of the EU’s supports to Agriculture Development in Ghana to be implemented with the resources mobilised under 11th EDF (2014 – 2020) as from 2017 onwards.

In this regard, a fully-fledged Action Document must be produced with annexes, all in line with procedures and standards prevailing for such an Action.

Expert in Resilient Agriculture (cat II) 42 working days

The expert must be graduated in Environment/Natural resources management, agro/socioeconomy or engineering studies linked to the above topics, with at least a Master Degree. Alternatively, he may have an equivalent professional experience of at least 2 years on top of the general required experience.

General required experience: The expert must have at least 6 years of general experience in sustainable agriculture or sustainable environment programmes, or any other related activities aiming at implementing actions in resilient agriculture in an African context.

Specific required experience: 1/ Fields to be covered: Evaluation/management of Development Projects on minimum 2 among the 3 below-listed sectors:

  • Agriculture development/rural development;
  • Environment protection/ management of natural resources/resilience;
  • Climate adaptation/mitigation.

2/ At least one field of the list below constitutes a minimum requirement (all fields will be scored maximum):

  • Expertise in promoting capacity building, local knowledge development, community development through cooperation programmes in Africa, particularly sub-Saharan Africa,;
  • Formulation of programmes (ability for reasoning and strategizing ideas).
  • In-depth knowledge of EDF procedures;

Experience in *Ghana *will be considered as an asset.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to lina_forgeaux@dai.com
