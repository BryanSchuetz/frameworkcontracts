Country Gender Profile

Objective

To conduct an overall gender analysis in order to provide an overview of the gender inequalities that persist in Zimbabwe and its causes, how it intersects with other inequalities, how it impacts on human rights and enjoyment and/or benefits produced by and access to development efforts as well as an understanding of the Government’s commitment and capacity to work on GEWE issues.

Specific objective

  • To collate the available information on the gender equality situation in Zimbabwe in order to produce a comprehensive country gender profile that will assist the EU Delegation (EUD) and EU Member States in identifying the most appropriate objectives to be tackled, from the three thematic priorities identified in the GAP in order to contribute to enhanced GEWE in Zimbabwe.
  • To inform the EUD and Member States' programming processes by providing practical recommendations on how to mainstream gender in the programmes being developed in the NIP focal areas of governance (justice, elections, peace building, culture), health, agriculture and natural resources management, as well as develop gender equality indicators for these focal areas.

Junior Gender Expert (20 working days) Qualifications and skills

  • University degree or equivalent with relevant work experience in Sociology, Development Studies, Gender studies or equivalent;
  • Good communication skills, both oral and written;
  • Computer literacy (minimum MS Word, Excel, Management Information Systems.

General professional experience

  • Minimum 3 years extensive experience in the context of gender and development and/ or as a consultant from both short and long-term assignments on gender related studies;
  • Extensive knowledge of the development context of Zimbabwe

Additional Team requirements

  • One of the experts must have expertise in participatory methods.
  • Experience in data analysis is a plus.

Working languages All experts must be fluent in oral and written English

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to CorinaMaria_Campian@dai.com
