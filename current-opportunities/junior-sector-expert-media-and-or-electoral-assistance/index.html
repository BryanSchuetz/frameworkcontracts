Junior Sector Expert (Media and/or Electoral Assistance)

  • Project: Evaluation of EU Democracy Assistance
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: 26 working days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/350646 - Version 1
  • Location: Georgia (Tbilisi and home-based)
  • Estimated Start Date: 1 April 2014
  • Application Deadline: 27 November 2014

The main objectives of the evaluation are:

  • to identify key lessons and to produce recommendations in order to improve the current and future European Union’s strategies, programmes and actions in the field of democracy support in general. In particular, the evaluation should provide lessons in order to inform the choice and preparation of actions under the Democracy Action Programme through programmes to be launched by the EU in Georgia in the period 2014 â€“ 2017;
  • to provide the relevant external co-operation services of the European Union and the wider public with an overall independent assessment of the European Union’s past and current cooperation with Georgia in the democracy field.

Junior Sector Expert (Media and/or Electoral Assistance)

An advanced degree in Political Science, Public Administration, International Development, or other related areas or equivalent professional experience of at least 5 years in in the field of democratic governance;

  • Good written and spoken English;

  • Knowledge of Georgian or Russian

  • At least three years of working experience in areas of democratic governance with an emphasis on either electoral assistance or media development;

  • Solid knowledge of institutional capacity development issues, preferably within a democratic governance framework;

  • Experience of at least one project involving capacity development of democratic governance stakeholders such as, inter alia, civil society organisations, media organisations, election management bodies, etc.;

  • Knowledge of democratic development over the past decade in Georgia would be an asset.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to chloe_messenger@dai.com
