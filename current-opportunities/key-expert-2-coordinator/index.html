Support to the EU-India Climate Change Dialogue

Global objective

The overall objective of the action is to pave the way for a possible EU-India Climate Change Dialogue and Partnership.

Specific objective(s):

The specific objectives of this assignment are:

  • To raise awareness on EU positions and know how in Climate Change and low carbon technologies by engaging Indian stakeholders such as NGOs, think tanks, businesses and Government officials.
  • To gather intelligence on policies and needs in view of facilitating European business and policy-level involvement in India’s Climate Change agenda
  • To initiate cooperative action between the EU and India, in support of mitigation, adaptation and transparency targets of the Paris Agreement.

Key Expert 2 –Coordinator (Category II)

The coordinator, appointed on a half time basis throughout the contract, will support the Team Leader and Short-term experts for the identification of relevant materials and stakeholders, establishing and maintaining contacts with Indian stakeholders, stakeholder consultations, organisation of meetings consolidation of outputs etc. This expertise should be available on the local market.

Qualifications and Skills:

  • Master’s Degree or equivalent in a field relevant to the project, or in its absence, a bachelor degree with an equivalent professional experience of at least five (5) years on top of the general experience required below.

General Professional Experience:

  • At least six (6) years of experience in the sector(s) related to the lot 6.

Specific Professional Experience:

  • At least three (3) years of working experience in India on climate actions and/or clean energies.
  • At least two (2) successful experiences working with the Indian public sector on climate change and/or mitigation

Language Skills:

  • Excellent command of English and Hindi is required, with a level that allows the interaction with all relevant stakeholders.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to CorinaMaria_Campian@dai.com
