Key expert 3: Expert for Industrialization and Value Addition; Category I

  • Project: To support the Mid-term Review of Uganda's 2nd National Development Plan (NDP-2) and evaluation of NDP-1
  • Lot: SIEA 2
  • Assignment Duration: 90 Working Days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2018/399295 - Version 1
  • Location: Uganda
  • Estimated Start Date: 1st September 2018
  • Application Deadline: 1st August 2018

Description of Project:

GLOBAL OBJECTIVE To contribute to the Government of Uganda’s successful completion of the Mid-Term Review (MTR) of the 2nd National Development Plan (NDP2) and the end evaluation of the 1st National Development Plan (NDP-1).

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

  • To assess progress made against the NDP-1 and NDP-2 objectives in the thematic areas of Infrastructure (1), Employment (2) Industrialization and Value Addition (3), Minerals, Oil and Gas (4) and Tourism (5).
  • To identify evidence-based, justified and detailed short-term (NDP-2), medium-term (NDP-3) and long-term (10 years NDP) adjustments and actions.

REQUESTED SERVICES

The Consultantsâ€™ services will indicatively include but not necessarily be limited to the following:

GENERAL SERVICES

  • Validate or update the specific organization & methodology, including work-plan, during the inception phase.
  • Identify, review and analyse reference documents that are relevant to the NDP2 MTR and the NDP1 end evaluation.
  • Undertake fieldwork to collect data and carry out data analysis to inform the respective assessment.
  • Conduct meetings with key stakeholders at various levels.
  • Conduct consultation and validation workshops to inform the outputs of the assignment.
  • Prepare an inception report
  • Prepare for each (5) thematic area a main report and a synthesis report.
  • Participate and take a leading role in the Government of Uganda’s dissemination conference(s) of the final reports
  • Establish a web-based repository of working documents to allow NPA to monitor timely progress made against the assignment’s outputs.

SPECIFIC SERVICES

  • Analyse for each of the 4 thematic areas outlined in chapter 2.2 the (a) current situation, (b) progress made against the NDP targets and © public and private financing against NDP projections.
  • Conduct for each thematic area an inventory (status) of public investment projects implemented during NDP.
  • Assess community-level experiences and perceptions.
  • Analyse for each thematic area main drivers and opportunities.
  • Identify for each thematic area the factors that have proved critical or hindered progress under both NDP-1 and NDP-2.
  • Assess for each thematic area the extent to which NDP-1 and NDP-2 have guided policies, strategic planning, budgeting and actions.
  • Assess for each thematic area the impacts (positive and negative) of policy, planning and budget actions under the NDP-1 and NDP-2 periods.
  • Assess the institutional functions linked with each thematic area under a programme-based planning and budgeting approach and provide the necessary adjustments to facilitate and enhance compliance between the NDP and the programme-based approaches. Apply for each thematic area the applicable OECD DAC Evaluation Criteria to NDP-1 and NDP-2.
  • Assess the efficiency, effectiveness and sustainability of the implementation and mechanisms for each thematic area under both the NDP-1 and the NDP-2 and outline the necessary improvements.
  • Analyse for each thematic area lessons learned during the NDP implementation.
  • Identify for each thematic area international best practices relevant to Uganda.
  • Identify and justify for each thematic area priority lessons learned and recommendations for the medium- (NDP-3) and long-term (10-year) NDP.
  • Provide for each thematic area evidence-/best practices-based guidance on alternative policies, strategic priorities and actions to achieve better results within Ugandaâ€™s setting.
  • Provide for each thematic area medium-term (2020/21-2024/25) and long-term (2020/21- 2029/30) estimates of required results and indicative budget requirements.
