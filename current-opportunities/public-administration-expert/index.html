Three Category I Experts in Public Administration Reform, HR Management in Civil Service and Civil Service Reform

  • Project: Advisory Support to Ukraine's Public Administration Reform Implementation
  • Lot: BENEF 7

  • Eligible Nationalities: All

  • Location: Ukraine
  • Estimated Start Date: 13th June 2016
  • Application Deadline: 18th May 2016

Advisory Support to Ukraine’s Public Administration Reform Implementation

Global objective

To improve accountability, quality, effectiveness and efficiency of the Ukrainian public administration on the basis of Ukraine’s Strategy of Public Administration Reform and its Implementation Plan.

Specific objectives:

To assist the Government of Ukraine in the process of launching the PAR Strategy, including establishment of relevant strategic framework, coordination structures and monitoring mechanism; To assist the Government in initial implementation of specific actions of the PAR Strategy through coaching and provision of conceptual, methodological and operational guidance based on the principles of public administration and best European practices. This shall cover reorganization of public administration, policy making reform and civil service reform.

Three Category I Experts in Public Administration Reform, HR Management in Civil Service and Civil Service Reform

Qualifications and skills:

  • Master degree in a field related to the assignment (law, public administration, management, economy) or equivalent professional experience (7 years of experience in public sector); Excellent English communication skills, both spoken and written;
  • Knowledge of Ukrainian (or Russian) will be an asset.

General professional experience

  • a minimum of 12 years experience in civil service or public sector following the award of the above required Master degree;

Specific professional experience: At least 8 years of specific and practical experience (following the award of the above required Master degree) in the fields related to the assignment:

  • Expert I: design and implementation of large scale public administration reforms, including overall coordination of PAR process, reorganisation of public administration, policy making reform
  • Expert II: HR management in civil service, civil service reform, including reform of salary system and design of relevant information management systems;
  • Expert III: design and implementation of large scale public administration reforms, including reorganisation of public administration, cost analysis of PAR

As part of 8 years of experience required above, at least 3 years of experience at senior level in the EU Member State’s administration or in the administration of an accession country in the fields related to the assignment.

While analysing specific professional experience, a particular attention will be paid to the following issues:

Expert 1

  • practical experience in the area of PAR at strategic level;
  • experience in drafting I implementing large scale reforms of public sector or I and public administration;
  • experience of preparing policy and legal assessments, comparative analysis in the area of PAR;
  • experience in facilitating high level policy dialogue in the area of PAR.

Expert 2

  • practical experience in the area of civil service reform at strategic level;
  • experience in drafting I implementing civil service reform, including drafting legal,policy (conceptual) and methodological documents;
  • experience in designing I implementing large scale IT projects related to HRM in civil service;
  • knowledge of European principles of public administration with a particular focus on civil service.

Expert 3

  • practical experience in the area of PAR at strategic level;
  • experience in drafting I implementing large scale reforms of public sector or /and public administration;
  • experience in carrying out cost analysis of PAR and its main components
  • experience in facilitating high level policy dialogue in the area of reorganisation of public administration;
  • experience in carrying out functional review of public bodies;

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to CorinaMaria_Campian@dai.com
