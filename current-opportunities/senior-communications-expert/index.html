Senior Communications Expert

  • Project: Pakistan: Support to Democratic Institutions
  • Lot: BENEF 7
  • Assignment Duration: 48 months
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: Pakistan/Comms
  • Location: Pakistan
  • Estimated Start Date: April 2015
  • Application Deadline: 20 December 2014

The Communications Expert shall lead in the advice, conceptualisation, implementation and oversight of actions related to Result 3 of this project relating to improving the transparency and accessibility of assemblies. The expert will lead on the development and implementing communication strategies, increasing the Assemblies' capabilities to disseminate information to the public for more participatory governance and to deliver timely information and documents to the members. The expert will work closely with the Secretariat’s respective branches, Parliamentary leadership, Committees, media. Together with the other experts, the expert will also assist on effective parliamentary and Committees' engagement with the media and the public (civil society, lawyers, bar associations, think tanks) for provision of input into parliamentary legislative and oversight business) and for improving the quantity and quality of media repotting on parliamentary business.

Senior Communications Expert

Qualifications and Skills:

  • University degree at Masters' level in media and communication, public relations, law, in social and/or political science, public administration or related discipline with experience of at least 15 years
  • Excellent leadership, management, communication (written and oral), networking and reporting skills
  • Excellent analytical skills and ability to conceptualise policy formulation within the subject area
  • Ability to work at senior parliamentary level; ability to work under pressure and to meet strict deadlines
  • Proficiency in written and oral English language

General Professional Experience:

  • At least ten years professional experience working within media and/or communication and/or Public Relations (PR)
  • Experience with international donor-funded programmes or experience on management position preferred
  • Exposure to international parliamentary practices in the areas of communication/PR is an advantage

Specific Professional Experience:

  • In-depth understanding of and experience with media, in the sectors of communications as well as public relations
  • Experience in strategic communications, development and implementation of communication strategies and systems for the public administration, private sector and/or legislative sector (parliament)
  • Solid knowledge of digital media, communication and outreach, and web content management system and process
  • Understanding of information and communication technologies is an advantage
  • Experience in liaising with civil society, lawyers, bar associations, think tanks, public administration and others
  • Knowledge of the concept and practice of the representation function of the parliament involving timely and quality public outreach and public input into parliamentary business
