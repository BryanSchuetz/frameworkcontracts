Senior Engineering Expert (TL) (Cat. I)

  • Project: Review of Works Tender Technical Specifications
  • Lot: BENEF 1
  • Assignment Duration: 20 days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2015/367136 - Version 1
  • Location: Nigeria - Owerri, Niger Delta, Abuja
  • Estimated Start Date: 16 November 2015
  • Application Deadline: 23 October 2015

The overall objective of the project of which this contract will be a part is as follows:

  • To mitigate the conflict in the Niger Delta by addressing the main causes of the unrest and violence: bad governance, (youth) unemployment, poor delivery of basic services. Specifically the programme will contribute to poverty alleviation through the development and support given to local community development initiatives.

The purpose of this contract is as follows:

  • To review the proposed technical specifications for the Cassava and Oil-Palm Mills and ensure technical accuracy, performance efficiency and fair competition for equipment suppliers that comply with the rule of origin.

Senior Engineering Expert (TL) (Cat. I)

  • As a minimum the expert should have a Masters' degree in Engineering, Technology, Electrical Electronics or relevant/equivalent field. 15 years of experience in the relevant fields mentioned may be accepted in lieu of Master’s degree.
  • A minimum of 10 years' experience in implementation of sustainable livelihoods agriculture engineering programmes, and infrastructural development programmes. S/he must be excellent in spoken and written English.

The expert should have specific experiences in the following areas:

  • A minimum of 10 years' experience in engineering projects including field experience; in appropriate/intermediate/basic technology, knowledge of engineering design and installation of agriculture equipment;
  • At least 7 years' experience in international development programme implementation involving engineering (civil, electrical, water or agro-processing), and installation of equipment for processing of produce with experience in sub Saharan Africa or similar situation;
  • Knowledge of electrical circuit or mechanics will be an added advantage;
  • Knowledge of intermediate or basic technology or similar skills in livelihoods programme including quality control/checks;
  • Work experience in West-Africa is an advantage;
  • Familiarity with the EDF regulations and procedures will be an added advantage.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to nicte_garcia-soria@dai.com
