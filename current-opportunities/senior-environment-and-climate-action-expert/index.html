Framework Contracts

Senior Environment and Climate Action Expert

  • Project: Ex-ante evaluation of IPA II 2015-2020 in the sectors of Environment and Climate Action, Transport, Competitiveness and Innovation and Education, Employment and Social Policies
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: 18 working days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/352155 - Version 1
  • Location: Montenegro
  • Estimated Start Date: 8 December 2014
  • Application Deadline: 5 November 2014

The global objective of the ex-ante evaluation is to improve the performance of the EU financial assistance and support decision-making for strategies and assistance programmes formulation. The focus is on enhancing the relevance, effectiveness, efficiency, impact, sustainability and accountability of the EU Pre-accession assistance by means of studies and evaluations of policies and projects financed from any of the instruments whose implementation is delegated to the Directorate General of DG Enlargement.

Project location: Podgorica (Montenegro), with missions to other regions of Montenegro. Project results to be presented in Brussels

Expert 3: Environment and Climate Action Expert

The required minimum (unless explicitly specified otherwise) qualifications of the expert proposed is:

  • University Degree up to Master’s degree or equivalent in social sciences and/or environmental sciences and/or economy and related fields (or professional experience of minimum 15 years relevant to the assignment for senior expert).
  • The general expertise, whether it be academic or gained in the field, must cover all the relevant issues covered by the Operational programme
  • Proficiency in both written and spoken English (i.e. ability to work, communicate and draw up reports in English).
  • High computer literacy

General professional experience, skills and competences The required minimum (unless explicitly specified otherwise) general professional experience of the expert is:

  • Minimum 10 years of working experience on climate action and environmental issues
  • Proven experience with EU’s pre-accession policy and financial instruments (in at least 1 EU funded project) in the relevant sectors in Southeast European countries will be considered as an asset.

*Specific professional experience, skills and competences * The required minimum (unless explicitly specified otherwise) specific professional experience of the expert is:

  • At least 5 years of working experience in preferably projects related to the EU assistance, implementation, preparation or analysis/evaluation of programmes funded under IPA and/or Structural Funds investment programmes;
  • At least one experience in providing technical assistance for the programming of pre-accession funds in the areas concerned in Candidate countries or Member-States.
  • Participation in at least two ex-ante evaluations of operational programmes.
  • Fluency in written and spoken local language would be an asset.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to chloe_messenger@dai.com and Francesca_giordano@dai.com
