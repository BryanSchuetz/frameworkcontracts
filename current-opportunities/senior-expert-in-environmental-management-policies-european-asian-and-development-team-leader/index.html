Evaluation of the project proposals submitted under the SWITCH-Asia II Programme Restricted call for Proposals 2017

Global objective:

The global objective of this assignment is to support the Commission to identify the project proposals that contribute best to achieving the objectives of the grant component of the SWITCH-Asia II Programme.

Specific objective(s) :

The specific objective is to provide services to the European Commission linked to the administrative (data processing, ranking, and statistical follow-up) and the qualitative parts (technical and financial assessment of Concept Notes and Full Applications) of the evaluation procedure.

Senior Expert in Environmental Management Policies (European/Asian) and Development (Team Leader)

General Professional Experience University degree and 12 years' experience in a field directly related to environmental management and policy;

Specific Professional Experience

  • Proven experience in technical and financial assessments of EU funded programmes/projects;
  • Thorough understanding of European and/or Asian environment policies and developments, in particular in sustainable consumption and production, cleaner production, industrial pollution, waste management, water management, sustainable energy
  • Proven experience with EU programmes/projects;
  • 4 years' experience in co-ordination/leadership position;

Language Skills

  • Be fluent in English (preferably level 1, but minimum level 2).

Soft Skills:

  • Excellent analytical and judgement skills demonstrated by outputs of at least 2 similar exercises.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to CorinaMaria_Campian@dai.com
