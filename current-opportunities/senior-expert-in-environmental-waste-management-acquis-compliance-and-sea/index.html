Senior Expert in Environmental/Waste Management Acquis, Compliance and SEA

  • Project: Development of Strategic Environmental Assessment (SEA) for National Waste Management Plan in Montenegro
  • Lot: BENEF 6
  • Assignment Duration: 90 working days (November 2014 - June 2015)
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/350828 - Version 1
  • Location: Montenegro
  • Estimated Start Date: 10 November 2014
  • Application Deadline: 16 October 2014

The global objective of the assignment is to ensure, economically and socially sound and viable development of Montenegro in a long-term perspective with special focus on waste management with a view of promoting sustainable development.

The main objective is to provide a high level of protection of the environment, incorporating environmental considerations in the development (preparation and adoption) of plans and programs. These should be a systematic decision support process of evaluating the environmental consequences, on but not limited to land degradation, air quality, surface and ground water quality, in order to ensure they are fully included and appropriately addressed at the earliest appropriate stage of decision-making on par with economic and social considerations. Details on SEA are defined under the Directive 2001/42/EC. As a consequence, a SEA process is mandatory for waste management plans.

Senior Expert in Environmental/Waste Management Acquis, Compliance and SEA

Qualifications and skills:

  • Education of at least Master degree in the field of environmental and/or biotechnical, technical sciences, law, spatial planning, economy, or equivalent professional experience of at least 15 years in the field of environmental and waste management legislation, chemistry, spatial planning;
  • Excellent written and oral knowledge of English language.

General professional experience:

Minimum 12 years of experience in the relevant field that encompasses the following:

  • Design, development and/or implementation of environmental and/or waste management policies and strategies,
  • Support to national authorities in the implementation of environmental and waste management policies in line with EU acquis.

Specific professional experience :

To have at least 5 years' work experience in the following fields relevant for this assignment:

  • EU environmental and waste management acquis. Drafting of legal acts and transposition of EU environmental acquis into national legislative frameworks;
  • Assessing of environmental compliance through comparison of national, international and EU environmental and waste management legislation;
  • Having been engaged as expert on at least two projects related to preparation of the Strategic Environmental Assessments (SEA).

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to chloe_messenger@dai.com
