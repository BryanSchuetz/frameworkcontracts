  • Project: Formulation of industrialisation programme for the SADC Secretariat
  • Lot: BENEF 10

Formulation of Industrialisation Programme for the SADC Secretariat

The global objective of the assignment is to provide expert assistance to the SADC Secretariat and the EU Delegation to Botswana and SADC in order to complete the formulation of a Programme on “Support to Industrialisation and the Productive Sectors”, taking into account the assistance provided to by other International Cooperation Partners (ICPs). The Programme is foreseen to be funded by the 11th EDF under the Annual Action programme 2017.

Location visits to: Tanzania, Mauritius, South Africa, Zimbabwe, Mozambique, Swaziland and DRC

*Senior Expert in Industrial Development – specifically Developing Countries/Emerging Markets (TL) – Cat. *I

  • Master’s degree preferably in Economics, International Trade, Industrial Development or equivalent or, in its absence, at least 3 years of professional experience in addition to the minimum professional experience required or, in its absence, at least 3 years of professional experience in addition to the minimum professional experience required

At least 12 years’ experience in:

  • Leading teams
  • Implementation of Regional projects
  • Developing countries and emerging market experience
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Proven Negotiating skills with a variety of project stakeholders at all levels
  • Proven experience in value chains in developing context
  • Experience in desk review work of strategies, regional policies, etc with specific focus on industrial development including value chains and value addition in the African context or, in its absence, in other developing countries and/or emerging markets;
  • Expertise and experience in analytical and presentation skills.
  • Good knowledge of WTO TBT and SPS agreements as well as EPA agreement;

  • Fluency in English (written, spoken, understanding).

  • Working knowledge of Portuguese and French would be a significant advantage.

  • Knowledge of SADC and its institutions as well as knowledge of the Revised RISDP, the SADC Industrialization Strategy and its Action Plan as well as other regional and continental initiatives on industrialization would be a significant advantage

  • Previous experience in drafting Action Documents (identification, formulation and action fiche) under the European Development Fund would be a significant advantage

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to [LinaLina_Forgeaux@dai.com](mailto:lina_forgeaux@dai.com)
