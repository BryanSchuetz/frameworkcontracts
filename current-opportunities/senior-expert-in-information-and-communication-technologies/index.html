Preparation of supply tender dossiers for Serbian Ministry of Interior (MUP)

Global objective

The overall objective of this assignment is to support Serbia’s commitment in meeting EU standards in the field of safety and security through implementation of Action Plan on Chapter 24 and Chapter 23.

Specific objective(s)

The specific objectives of this assignments is to provide preliminary assessments, market researches, preparation of technical specifications and tender dossiers and providing input during clarifications and in the tender evaluations for the following tender procedures:

  • Supply of ICT equipment, security equipment, software solutions and analytical tools for strengthening capacities within Home Affairs Sector for the fight against organized crime by implementation of: Intelligence Led Policing, National Criminal Intelligence System and Strengthening international police cooperation (in line with EUROPOL standards)
  • Supply of video, audio and IT equipment for strengthening capacity of Internal Control Unit to fight against corruption within the police

Expert 2 Expert in Information and Communication Technologies - Category 1

Qualifications and skills:

  • University degree /Master Degree (where a university degree has been awarded on completion of four years study or equivalent institutions) in the field of engineering, information and technology or other field related to this contract.

General professional experience:

  • At least twelve (12) years, preferably 15 years of working experience in the design of integrated ICT and networking systems on multi-vendor platform
  • Fluency in English, both written and spoken

Specific professional experience:

  • At least 1, preferably 2 projects in consulting experience with public sector organizations in the area of design and implementation of complex ICT infrastructure systems (such as data centres, data back-up, archive and restore, basic network and communication systems, CLOUD technology, security of information systems – identity management, smart cards, RFID technologies, risk management etc.)
  • Experience in at least one (1) ICT project within Home Affaires sector in the area of design and implementation of security and protection systems for complex ICT and Networking infrastructure is an advantage
  • Proven experience acquired within the last 6 calendar years in at least one (1) project related to implementation and managing of continuity of service in ICT system due to be available 24/7/365 is an advantage.
  • Experience in preparation of technical specifications for 1, preferably 2, IT supply projects.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to aurelie_durand@dai.com
