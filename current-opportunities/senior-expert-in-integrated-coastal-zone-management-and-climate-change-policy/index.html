Final Evaluation of the GCCA Support Project to the Gambia

Overall objective of this project is to contribute to the capability of the government and people of The Gambia to adapt to increased climate variability and change.

Senior Expert in Integrated Coastal Zone Management and Climate Change Policy (Cat. I)

  • Master’s degree of equivalent in the area relevant to the assignments such as environmental sciences, climate change, management of natural resources, land management and planning, geography or economy or relevant professional experience of 3 years duration (above the general professional experience below);
  • General professional experience of at least 12 years in an area relevant to the assignment;
  • Proven international experience with evaluations, sector assessment, project/programme planning and execution and development exercises in the field of Integrated Coastal Zone Management and Climate Change Policy;
  • An experience in the EU sector policies and strategic orientations and practical knowledge of the EU cooperation in the area relevant to the assignment;
  • Experiences in least developed countries with fragile institutional context;
  • Fluent in English
