Senior Expert in Public Health, Health Policy and Planning

  • Project: Mid-term Evaluation of the Programme in support for the Health Sector II
  • Lot: SIEA 4
  • Assignment Duration: 2 months
  • Eligible Nationalities: all
  • Project Reference: 2018/399027 - Version 1
  • Location: Angola
  • Estimated Start Date: August 2018
  • Application Deadline: 9th July 2018

Mid-term Evaluation of the Programme in support for the Health Sector II

The consultants will provide clear advice on the Program´s outcomes and the sustainability of the actions and tools developed as part of PASS II. He will conduct a sound analysis of the Program within the health sector in Angola in order to assess key factors influencing sustainability issues and produce recommendations to reinforce the sustainability and the ownership by MINSA. They will assess the effectiveness of the Program´s interventions in achieving the goal and objectives set in the original Financing Agreement, which are:

  • Overall objective: to contribute to improved health conditions of the population, especially women and children, with a focus in the provinces of Huambo, Bié, Benguela, Huíla e Luanda.
  • Specific objectives: 1: To strengthen MOH capacity, at central level, in strategic and operational planning, budgeting, organization of the health system, training and management of Human Resources for Health, coordination, advocacy and monitoring and evaluation; 2: To strengthen Provincial Health Directorates capacity and leadership in planning, budgeting, development of the health system (including training and management of HRH) and management of activities, in accordance with the ongoing administrative decentralization and the Revitalization of the Municipal Health System processes; 3: To improve the accessibility and quality of the basic package of health services provided to the population at municipal level, by reinforcing the availability of good quality essential maternal, newborn and children health services, with a specific focus in the provinces of Huambo, Bie, Benguela, Huíla and Luanda.

Senior Expert in Public Health, Health Policy and Planning

  • At least 12-year experience in Public Health.
  • MSc in Public Health, Health Policy and Planning, Health Services Management, or equivalent from a recognized University.
  • Professional experience of at least 12 years as public health expert / consultant in developing countries.
  • Experience with at least 3 different organizations in conducting health systems reviews, health sector reforms, strategic planning and health services evaluations.
  • Background working in post conflict environments and fragile states in the health sector.Experience working with EU funded programs: EDF, Global Budget, etc.
  • Professional experience in Angola.
  • Familiarity and experience with EU programs of support to the health sector in Angola and other countries. Strong analytical and synthesis skills.
  • Negotiation skills to develop consensus around policy strategies.
  • Excellent leadership skills, ability to exercise a significant degree of initiative and to work effectively with stakeholders.
  • Ability to work with individuals and teams in completing tasks that are urgent, high priority and sensitive, and ability to deliver high quality work on tight timelines.
  • Strong interpersonal communications and writing skills
  • Fluency in Portuguese.
  • Excellent Portuguese written skills / English written skills desirable
