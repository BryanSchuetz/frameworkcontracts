Senior Experts in Migration with Research Experience

  • Project: Technical Assistance for Comprehensive Assessment Study on Mapping of Sources and Key Trends of International Migration
  • Lot: BENEF 7
  • Assignment Duration: 145 working days (for team as a whole)
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: ALTUN/TAAIM/TR2010/0740.01-2/FWC/006
  • Location: Turkey, Ankara
  • Estimated Start Date: October 2014
  • Application Deadline: 26 August 2014

*Global Objective * The global aim of this project is to accelerate Turkey’s accession to the EU by supporting the country’s policy makers and practitioners in designing and implementing evidence based comprehensive and realistic policies regarding international migration in Turkey.

*Specific Objective(s) * The specific objective of this project is to propose a methodology and assessment tools for analyzing migratory trends and characteristics; migration governance framework, reviewing international cooperation initiatives and regional context as well as migration impacts which will serve as basis of annual migration reports to be published by the General Directorate of Migration Management.

Project location: Ankara and field visits to three districts in the provinces out of Ankara.

Senior Experts in Migration with Research Experience

Maximum 3 Senior experts that cover the following requirements to successfully deal with the issues and to cover all the activities indicated in this Terms of Reference.

Each proposed expert should meet following requirements:

  • At least Masters’ degree on social sciences, law or related fields. In the absence of the required Masters degree, the expert will have an additional equivalent professional experience of minimum 17 years in the relevant fields.
  • At least 12 years of professional working experience in the sector(s) related to the lot.
  • At least 2 years’ experience in quantitative and qualitative research methodology
  • At least 3 years’ experience in international migration.
  • Good command of written and spoken English.

The proposed experts shall cover the following specific experiences as a team:

  • Experience in the preparation of a situation assessment and yearly reports.
  • Experience in a project or research related to the labor policies and economics analyses.
  • Experience in a project or research requiring techniques in data mining and/or handling collected data.
  • Experience in a project or research related to impact assessment methodologies.
  • Experience in a project or research related to data collection and management on migration.
  • Experience in a project or research utilizing SWOT analyses or equivalent management tool..

The experts shall satisfy the criteria as a team but each expert shall at least cover one of the requirements.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to chloe_messenger@dai.com
