Senior Finance Expert with Emerging/Frontier Markets Experience (TL)

  • Project: Technical Assistance And Study To Define Parameters Of A Convertible Grants Support Scheme
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: Up to 70 working days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/353676 - Version 1
  • Location: Brussels and home-based
  • Estimated Start Date: 5 January 2015
  • Application Deadline: 1 December 2014

In the context of implementing the Annual Action Plan of 2014 for sustainable energy under the Global Public Goods And Challenges (GPGC) 2014-2020 the EC will finance risk capital in projects aiming at increasing and improving access to modern, affordable and sustainable energy services for poor populations.

The objective of the assignment is to contribute in successfully implementing the Action through a convertible grants scheme that is used for the first time in the EU development cooperation in Energy.

Senior Finance Expert with Emerging/Frontier Markets Experience (TL)

Requested services: S/he will have the lead role in modelling the interaction of the team with the stakeholders and in structuring the information needed to deliver the set of guide documents, and will, as per Commission instructions, also interface with the Lead EDFI and/or the selected EDFIs group of representatives preparing for/entrusted with the Action’s budget-implementation tasks. S/he will report to the EC and will be responsible for the overall operational effectiveness and for certifying that the EC policies, procedures and processes are well understood and duly incorporated in the deliverables by the contract team, while ensuring that the experts act in a cohesive manner. S/he will also be in charge of the quality assurance of the documents.

Qualifications and skills: Master degree or equivalent in Banking/Finance, Economics, or other relevant equivalent education in fields related to the tasks to be carried out.

Professional experience: The Team Leader appointed will be a finance expert with 10 years of general professional experience in the field. S/he will also have at least 3 years’ experience in developing and/or emerging markets and/or frontier markets, with proven experience in the area of leveraging international financing for investment projects. S/he should have a sound record of consultancy services provided to Companies1 on strategies and investment opportunities. The Team leader will have proven experience (i) in negotiating investments/business deals between investors/developers and financiers and (ii) in managing multifunctional international experts’ teams.

Work experience on renewable energy investments will be an advantage. Work experience in renewable energy investments in sub-Saharan Africa will be a particular advantage.

Proficient knowledge of English (both oral and written). Excellent communication and presentation skills.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to chloe_messenger@dai.com
