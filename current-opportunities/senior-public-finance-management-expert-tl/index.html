Final Evaluation of LGU PFM Project

Global objective

The global objective of the assignment is to report on progress and results achieved during implementation compared to the initial objectives of the programme as well as lessons learnt.

Systematic and timely evaluation of its programmes and activities is an established priority of the European Commission. The focus of evaluations is on the assessment of achievements, the quality and the results of interventions in the context of an evolving cooperation policy with an increasing emphasis on result-oriented approaches. Evaluations should provide an understanding of the cause and effects links between activities and results. Evaluations should serve decision making, learning and management purposes.

Specific objective(s) The specific objectives of the evaluation are to provide the relevant external co-operation services of the European Union, the partner government and, when appropriate, the wider public with:

  • an overall independent assessment of the past performance of the intervention “Support to the Local Government Units for more Effective and Accountable Public Finance Management”, paying particularly attention to the results of the project against its objectives;
  • key lessons and recommendations in order to improve sustainability and mainstreaming of the results.

Expert 1 : Public Finance Management Expert -Team leader
