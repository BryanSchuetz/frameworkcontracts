Support to the preparation of Terms of Reference for IPA 2014 Project ‘Support to the Customs Administration’

The overall objective of the assignment is to assure the fulfilment of the EU accession requirements concerning the Chapter 29 (Customs Union) and provide support to the Montenegrin authorities in the accession to the CTC and SAD Conventions and implementation of NCTS.

The specific objective of this global price contract is the engagement of two experts for the review and update of the two Terms of Reference (hereinafter: ToRs) for the IPA 2014 Project “Support to the Customs Administration”, which have been already drafted, which aims at strengthening the operational capacity of the Customs Administration of Montenegro (MCA) in order to implement national customs transit procedures fully aligned to the requirements of Common external and internal transit procedures of the European Union and in order to implement National Transit Application (NTA) fully aligned to the requirements of the New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) latest current phase and version.

Team Leader and Business Expert

Business Expert should have a broad general knowledge of all processes and issues related to functioning of the Custom Administration within the EU. Business Expert will be responsible for overall coordination and implementation of the project activities, also directly participating in such activities as required. Team leader shall be also responsible for submission of reports to the Contracting Authority and the main contact point of the Contracting Authority. The expert shall have excellent commend of the English language, ability to ensure good communication and coordination with the Beneficiary, the Contracting Authority and other partners and stakeholders, as well as Excellent presentation, communication, reporting and drafting skills. The expert shall supervise and co-ordinate all technical aspects of a contract.

Qualifications and skills

  • Education at least Masters Degree in Economics, IT, Public administration, Business Administration or other areas relevant to the project; or, in its absence, equivalent professional experience of at least 15 years in the field of Trade facilitation/customs.
  • To have full working knowledge of EU Customs standards and legislation and of the Convention on Common Transit/NCTS;
  • Excellent knowledge of EU best practices in Customs business;

General professional experience:

  • At least 12 years of experience in Trade facilitation/customs, out of which minimum 8 years in IT system implementation for Custom Administrations within an EU Member States or accession country Customs Administration

Specific professional experience:

  • Minimum 5 years of working experience in IT system implementation in the fields related to the Common Transit Convention/NCTS system in EU Member States, European Commission Services or candidate/potential candidate countries.
  • To have experience in at least 1 project in the past 5 years in developing similar documents and implementation of NCTS related projects;

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to nicte_garcia-soria@dai.com
