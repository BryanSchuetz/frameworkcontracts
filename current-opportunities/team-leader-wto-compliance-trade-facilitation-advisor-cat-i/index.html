Team Leader/WTO Compliance/Trade Facilitation Advisor (Cat. I)

  • Project: Provision of Technical Assistance to support the Ministry of Economic Development and Trade of the Republic of Tajikistan in Post-WTO Accession Activities
  • Lot: BENEF 10
  • Assignment Duration: 150 days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2015/363859 - Version 1
  • Location: Tajikistan
  • Estimated Start Date: 2nd October 2015
  • Application Deadline: 5th August 2015

The global objective of this assignment is to contribute to the fulfilment of Tajikistanâ€™s commitments taken during the WTO accession.

The specific objective of the assignment is to provide support for the implementation of the post-WTO accession Action Plan approved by the Government of Tajikistan, most notably the action items related to the following:

  • Obtaining the GSP Plus status;

  • Becoming signatory of the WTO Agreement on Trade in Civil aircraft;

  • Submitting ITA Schedule to the WTO ITA Committee;

  • Negotiations for the accession to WTO Agreement on Government Procurement;

  • Technical support to and capacity building of SUE staff in operating the SWIS.

Team Leader/WTO Compliance/Trade Facilitation Advisor (Cat. I)

  • Masters' Degree of Academic level in law, economics, international trade or political science from a recognized university or more than 15 years of equivalent professional experience;
  • Fluency in English, oral and written;
  • S/he should have at least 12 years' of professional experience related to international trade and/or international law;
  • Solid experience in trade related technical assistance, demonstrated by at least five years of experience in implementing trade assistance programmes;
  • In-depth knowledge and expertise of the multilateral trading system, most notably WTO, demonstrated by his/her participation in at least two long term (of at least one year each) assignments in support to trade negotiations within the WTO framework;
  • Proven leadership and people management skills, including the ability to establish and maintain effective working relationships with others in an international multi-cultural working environment, as demonstrated through the participation in at least one long-term assignment (of minimum one year) as Team Leader;
  • Familiarity with rural Tajikistan, or a similar transitional context, is considered as an asset;
  • Knowledge of Russian and/or Tajik language is considered to be an asset;
  • Previous experience in EU-funded programmes and projects would be an asset.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to nicte_garcia-soria@dai.com
