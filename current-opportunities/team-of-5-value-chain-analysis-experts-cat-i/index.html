Technical Assistance to the establishment of a baseline of employment in Rwanda’s Export-oriented Agricultural Value Chains

Global Objective

  • Support the Government of Rwanda in its efforts to increase the contribution of export-oriented agricultural value chains to the country’s poverty reduction, economic- and export growth targets.

Specific Objective

  • Analyse and estimate the distribution of employment within and between various levels of Rwanda’s traditional (coffee, tea, pyrethrum) export-oriented agricultural value chains;

  • Analyse and estimate the distribution of employment within and between various levels of Rwanda’s non-traditional (especially horticulture and if possible hide and skins) export-oriented agricultural value chains;

  • Analyse the dynamics of employment within and along the above mentioned value chains and inclusion of women, youth, poor and disadvantaged groups;

  • Analyse the potential impact of different value chain governance; and value chain upgrading strategies on the employment distribution of Rwanda’s export-oriented agricultural value chains.

  • Define the baseline for the employment in the value chains analysed and a methodology for monitoring progress in the subsequent years.

Team of 5 Value Chain Analysis Experts (Cat. I)

  • At least 12 years successful experience in a field related to the implementation of this contract.
  • Proven English language report writing and communication skills.
  • At least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional experience in the survey and analysis of the employment distribution within agricultural value chains.
  • At least one expert with at least 12 years of successful professional experience in measuring and/or analysing decent rural employment, including in the informal sector.
  • At least one expert with at least 6 years of successful professional experience in analysing the Coffee value chain.
  • At least one expert with at least 6 years of successful professional experience in analysing the Tea value chain.
  • At least one expert with profound experience in analysing the Pyrethrum value chain.
  • At least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional experience in analysing Fruit & Vegetable value chains.
  • At least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional experience in analysing Hide & Skins value chains.
  • t least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional experience in analysing Rwanda’s export-oriented agricultural value chains.
  • Expertise with other high-value chains (e.g. flowers, essential oils, Macadamia, Stevia, etc.), that are relevant for Rwanda, will be an asset.
