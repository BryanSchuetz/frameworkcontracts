Technical IP Network senior expert (Team Leader)

  • Project: Operational Support to Telecommunications NGN System, Cyprus
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: 125 working days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/348284 - Version 1
  • Location: Northern Cyprus
  • Estimated Start Date: 1 December 2014
  • Application Deadline: 12 October 2014

The overall objective of the telecom infrastructure project is to upgrade the telecommunication infrastructure by developing a supplementary telecommunication network that will be suitable for the market conditions of northern part of Cyprus and in line with the European Union standards. As such, it aims to set up an Internet Protocol (IP) based type of infrastructure, similar to the systems being employed by the leading communications providers in different countries in the European Union. This type of all-IP based networks is called Next Generation Networks (NGN) and can be used to provide different types of services (internet, telephony) using the same infrastructure.

Specific objectives

  • To provide the beneficiary with daily support and with know-how for operating the NGN solution in a sustainable way.
  • To provide the Contracting Authority with further assurance that the supplies delivered are: o properly used and kept in good conditions by the beneficiary; o properly supported and maintained by the supplier with a view to the delivery of Final Acceptance Certificate (FAC).
  • To provide the Contracting Authority with information about the running NGN solution with a view to the delivery of the Final Acceptance Certificate.

Technical IP Network senior expert (Team Leader)

  • University degree in relevant fields such as telecommunications, computer science,electrical engineering, electronics, etc..

  • Training and/or certification on Ericsson MPBN (Mobile Packet Backbone Network) or on equipment of similar functionality from other manufacturer (e.g. Cisco equivalent training for IP/MPLS (Internet Protocol/Multi-Protocol Label Switching) and IP Networking and IP Quality of Service).

  • Training and/or certification on EDA 2500 MSANs (Multi Service Access Nodes).

  • Training and/or certification on Redback router products.

  • Training and/or certification on Juniper firewall products.

  • Training and/or certification on one or more of the following: Ericsson IP Technology and/or CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) and/or CCNP (Cisco Certified Network Professional) and/or equivalent IP technology from other vendor(s).

Professional experience: Minimum requirement:

  • At least 10 years of professional experience in telecommunications sector

Preferably:

  • Professional experience as a Manager or Team Leader in wire and/or wireless broadband and/or IP telecommunications networks in either of the below: 1) Operation / Maintenance department 2) Network Management Centre. Professional experience on Ericsson Broadband Access Nodes (EDA portfolio). Specific experience on EDA 2500 MSANs would be of an advantage.
  • Professional experience on Ericsson Redback routers products.
  • Professional experience on general enterpris firewalls products. Specific experience on Juniper firewalls products would be of an advantage.
  • Professional experience on Ericsson NetOp EMS & PM and Ericsson ServiceOnElement Manager (SO-EM).
  • Professional experience with IP and Ethernet technologies including configuration,testing, provisioning and troubleshooting of routers and switches.
  • Professional experience in operating and/or troubleshooting NGN telecommunication solutions or IP-based telecommunication networks.
  • Professional experience in performing audits (including producing test procedures and using software based test tools) on all-IP based networks.
  • Professional experience in operating and/or troubleshooting network with IMS / MTAS technology.

Specific knowledge: Minimum requirement:

  • Very good knowledge of English

Preferably:

  • Working knowledge of IETF (Internet Engineering Task Force) and their RFC’s (Requests for Comments) standards.
  • Working knowledge of TCP/IP, Unix/Linux, Shell scripting.
