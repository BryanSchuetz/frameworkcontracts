Technical Transmission Senior Expert

  • Project: Operational Support to Telecommunications NGN System, Cyprus
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: 48 Working days
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2014/348284 - Version 1
  • Location: Northern Cyprus
  • Estimated Start Date: 1 December 2014
  • Application Deadline: 24 October 2014

The overall objective of the telecom infrastructure project is to upgrade the telecommunication infrastructure by developing a supplementary telecommunication network that will be suitable for the market conditions of northern part of Cyprus and in line with the European Union standards. As such, it aims to set up an Internet Protocol (IP) based type of infrastructure, similar to the systems being employed by the leading communications providers in different countries in the European Union. This type of all-IP based networks is called Next Generation Networks (NGN) and can be used to provide different types of services (internet, telephony) using the same infrastructure.

Specific objectives

  • To provide the beneficiary with daily support and with know-how for operating the NGN solution in a sustainable way.

  • To provide the Contracting Authority with further assurance that the supplies delivered are: o properly used and kept in good conditions by the beneficiary; o properly supported and maintained by the supplier with a view to the delivery of Final Acceptance Certificate (FAC).

  • To provide the Contracting Authority with information about the running NGN solution with a view to the delivery of the Final Acceptance Certificate.

Technical transmission senior expert

Education:

  • University degree in relevant fields such as telecommunications, computer science,electrical engineering, electronics, etc.
  • Training and/or certification on the installation and provisioning of transmission network elements, including DWDM (Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing) and SDH (Synchronous Digital Hierarchy).
  • Training and/or certification in OSI (Open System Interconnection) routing and network payload protocols.
  • Training and/or certification on installation and provisioning of Microwave Radio Systems.

Professional experience: Minimum requirement:

  • At least 10 years of professional experience in telecommunications sector

Preferably:

  • Professional experience as a Manager or Team Leader in wire and/or wireless broadband and/or IP telecommunications networks in either of the below: 1) Operation / Maintenance department 2) Network Management Centre.
  • Professional experience with Element Managers (EMs) including connectivity to network nodes on core and transmission parts.
  • Professional experience, including configuration, testing, provisioning and troubleshooting with the following nodes: Ericsson Optical Multi Service family and Ericsson Multihaul 3000 Product and Ericsson Mini Link products family or with their equivalent from other manufacturers.
  • Professional experience in operating and/or troubleshooting wire and/or wireless broadband telecommunication networks. Professional experience in operating and/or troubleshooting WiMax solutions. Specific experience on NetSpan WiMax would be of an advantage.
  • Professional experience, as operational field manager, responsible for troubleshooting and repair of wire and/or wireless transmission network faults.
  • Professional experience in performing audits (including producing test procedures and using software based test tools) on networks.
  • Professional experience on Ericsson ServiceOn Element Manager (SO-EM).

Specific knowledge: Minimum requirement:

  • Very good knowledge of English

Preferably:

  • Knowledge of ITU-T standards (International Telecommunication Union telecom network standards).

Working language The working language is English.
