Three Junior Experts in Communications/Journalism/Media

  • Project: Guidelines for EU support to media freedom and media integrity in the enlargement countries 2014-20
  • Lot: EC COM 1
  • Assignment Duration: maximum of 80 days over 7 months
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2015/958295- Version 1
  • Location: Turkey and Balkans
  • Estimated Start Date: 4 May 2015

Guidelines for EU support to media freedom and media integrity in the enlargement countries 2014-20

To assess the baseline values for each objective and result of the Guidelines against relevant indicators/ benchmarks in all the seven countries providing reliable aggregate data for the reference year 2014.

Location Visits: Albania, BiH, FYROM, Kosovo, Montenegro, Serbia and Turkey

Three Junior Experts in Communications/Journalism/Media

Minimum requirements:

Qualifications and skills:

University Degree in the field of social sciences, communications and journalism or in its absence, minimum 5 years of professional experience in the same fields. Excellent oral and writing skills in English (at least level 2 in a scale from 1 to 5, where 1 is “excellent” and 5 is “basic” (template of CV). Knowledge of one or more local languages (at least level 3 in a scale from 1 to 5, where 1 is “excellent” and 5 is “basic” (template of CV).

General professional experience:

  • Minimum of 2 years post-degree relevant professional experience in evaluation, monitoring of programme and policies or academic research. Proven knowledge of local media contexts in the Western Balkans and Turkey (e.g. publications, professional or academic experience).
  • The Contractor will make sure that the experts work in cooperation with each other. All experts will be independent and free of conflicts of interest. The team as a whole should have good command of the following languages Albanian, Serbo-Croat-Bosnian, Macedonian and Turkish.

Please do not apply unless you speak at least one of the above languages.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to chloe_messenger@dai.com
