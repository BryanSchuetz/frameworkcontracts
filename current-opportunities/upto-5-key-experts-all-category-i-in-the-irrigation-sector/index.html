Technical Assistance in the Establishment of a Baseline of Beneficiaries of Public Investments in Irrigation Infrastructure

GLOBAL OBJECTIVE

Support the Government of Rwanda in its efforts to increase the efficiency, effectiveness, sustainability, leverage and impact of public investments in irrigation and to enhance the agriculture sectors' contribution to Rwanda’s poverty reduction-, economic growth- and export growth targets and to decrease farmer’s vulnerability to climate change, drought and flooding.

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

  • Establish a baseline of agricultural households using irrigation systems (their number, gender, perceptions, plot size under irrigation, cultivated crops, major challenges)

  • Complete the 2015 assessment of irrigation infrastructure built through public spending.

  • Complete the 2015 assessment of the use of irrigation infrastructure built through public spending.

  • Identify the cooperatives and water user associations accessing irrigation infrastructure.

  • Complete the 2015 assessment of the status, capacities and constraints of Water User Associations.

  • Support the Government of Rwanda in the country-wide assessment of the existing water storage structures with a minimum size of 120m³ or above.

  • Identify targeted policy actions to determine and improve water and land productivity in the agriculture sector.

  • Define a methodology for monitoring progress in the subsequent years and a periodicity.

Project duration: 180 days

Upto 5 Key Experts (all Category I) in the Irrigation Sector (total of 200 working days)

For all Experts the following requirements should be fulfilled:

Qualifications and skills: Education at least Masters Degree or, in its absence, equivalent professional experience in a field related to the implementation of this contract.

General professional experience: At least 12 years successful experience (for category I experts) or 6 years successful experience (for category II experts) in a field related to the implementation of this contract.

Language Skills: Proven English language report writing and communication skills.

In addition the team should cover all together the following requirements:

Professional experience:

  • At least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional experience in large surveys related to irrigated agriculture.
  • At least one expert with at least 12 years of successful professional in ensuring the sustainable management transfer (Water User Associations and/or private operators) of irrigation infrastructure and in supporting capacity building and capacity assessment of Water User Associations.
  • At least one expert with at least 12 years of successful professional in the economic appraisal and of public investments in irrigation infrastructure.
  • At least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional in the design and construction of irrigation infrastructure.
  • At least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional in analysing the hydrology for irrigation investment projects.
  • At least one expert with at least 12 years of successful professional in the environmental appraisal of public investments in irrigation infrastructure.
  • At least one expert with at least 12 years of successful professional in the social appraisal and of public investments in irrigation infrastructure.
  • At least one expert with at least 8 years of successful professional experience with irrigation schemes and water storage systems in Rwanda.
  • At least one expert with profound knowledge of the key stakeholders in Rwanda’s irrigation and water resources management sector.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to CorinaMaria_Campian@dai.com
