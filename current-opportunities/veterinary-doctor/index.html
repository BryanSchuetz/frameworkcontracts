Veterinary Doctor

  • Project: Assessment Mission on Animal Health in Timor Leste
  • Lot: BENEF 1
  • Assignment Duration: 15 working days over 4 months
  • Eligible Nationalities: All
  • Project Reference: 2018/395077 - Version 1
  • Location: Timor Leste
  • Estimated Start Date: 16th April 2018
  • Application Deadline: 07/02/2018 at 12:00 (noon) UK time

Assessment Mission on Animal Health in Timor Leste

Global objective The objective of the Framework Contract is to support MAF in developing of long term plan to control animal diseases in partnerships with development partners and neighbouring countries (Indonesia, Australia). The current assignment is to assess the current situation.

Specific Objectives The Framework Contractor is required to assess the animal health services currently provided by government (MAF) and private veterinarians. The Framework Contractor will provide a general assessment of the livestock sector with recommendations toward on integrated whole-of-Timor control programme. The assessment will include an overview of Development Partners actions regarding animal health.

Veterinary Doctor

  • Category I Expert with at least a Master Degree in Animal Production / Science / Veterinary Science or equivalent and at least 12-year of proven relevant professional experience or equivalent professional experience of at least 15 years1 in the sector, if the candidate does not possess a Master Degree relevant to Lot No. 1.
  • At least 5 previous professional experiences similar to this assignment, in particular the evaluation of the performance of veterinary services.
  • At least 2 professional experiences in the coordination with multi-donor environment and assessment of Development Partners’ interventions in the livestock sector.
  • At least 3 missions to analyse national veterinary services in ACP beneficiaries’ countries and or the in the APAC region (APAC: Australia, Bangladesh, Cambodia, China, Fiji, French Polynesia, Guam, Hong Kong, India, Japan, South Korea, Laos, Malaysia, Micronesia, Myanmar, New Zealand, Pakistan, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand and Vietnam.)

Language Skills

  • Proficiency in written and spoken Portuguese;

Desired Assets

  • Previous working experience in Asia;
  • Working knowledge of English and Bahasa

Note that civil servants and other staff of the public administration of the beneficiary country cannot be proposed as experts, unless prior written approval has been obtained from the European Commission.

If you meet the requirements and would be interested in applying for this role please email your CV in EC format to aurelie_durand@dai.com
